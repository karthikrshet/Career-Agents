# Career OS Case Studies

This file compiles detailed structural studies of candidates who utilized the Career Operating System to achieve their milestones.

## 📁 Case Study Index

### 📈 Case Study 1: The Non-Tech to SDE Career Switch
- **Candidate Background**: Sales Operations specialist at an insurance company.
- **Milestone Goal**: Transition to Frontend React Engineer.
- **Ecosystem Execution**:
  1. Ran `career-agents recommend` to match required competencies (matched CS Fundamentals, Git, JavaScript, CSS).
  2. Activated `career-switch-bundle` prompts. Used `career-pivot-to-tech-advisor` to establish a 6-month curriculum timeline.
  3. Integrated `ats-resume-reviewer` into Cursor to refactor the CV, focusing on transferable databases and automation achievements.
- **Result**: Hired as Junior Frontend Developer at a local fintech startup within 5 months.

### 🏢 Case Study 2: Clearing the Google L5 SDE Loop
- **Candidate Background**: Mid-level Infrastructure SRE at a regional logistics company.
- **Milestone Goal**: Clear Google Systems Design and Coding tracks.
- **Ecosystem Execution**:
  1. Ran `career-agents score` (identified gaps: Behavioral story details and systems design).
  2. Practiced System Design exercises using `system-design-coach` guidelines.
  3. Run Googleyness mock panels using `google-interview-coach` simulated sandbox mode.
- **Result**: Cleared the Google Software Engineer L5 loop with top-tier feedback on clean code communication.
