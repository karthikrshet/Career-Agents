# FAANG Preparation

## Objective
Create a focused, multi-week FAANG preparation system that turns technical practice, behavioral coaching, and offer strategy into a market-ready campaign.

## Target audience
- Developers targeting FAANG, top-tier tech, or equivalent elite engineering opportunities.
- Engineers who need a structured preparation plan for high-frequency technical screens.
- Candidates preparing for both coding and system-design interview sequences.

## Recommended agents
- `career/technical-interview-coach.md`
- `career/resume-strategist.md`
- `career/linkedin-growth-advisor.md`
- `career/offer-evaluation-advisor.md`
- `career/hr-interview-coach.md`

## Step-by-step process
1. Define target companies, roles, and timeline with your FAANG preparation goals.
2. Align your resume and public profile using `resume-strategist` to highlight impact, scale, and domain fit.
3. Use `technical-interview-coach` to build a practice plan for algorithms, system design, and code review.
4. Run mock behavioral interview sessions through `hr-interview-coach` with FAANG-style story frameworks.
5. Keep an interview journal to capture recurring themes, feedback, and questions.
6. Use `offer-evaluation-advisor` to compare compensation and role structure once multiple offers arrive.
7. Iterate weekly based on performance gaps and recruiter feedback.

## Weekly execution plan
Week 1: Define target companies, fix resume, and set up the practice schedule.
Week 2: Reinforce core data structures and algorithms; complete mock coding drills.
Week 3: Add system design prep and behavioral story refinement.
Week 4: Ramp interview simulations and review live test questions.
Week 5: Conduct full-day interview rehearsals and polish closing questions.
Week 6: Evaluate progress, negotiate terms, and solidify the final offer decision.

## Success metrics
- Number of FAANG-style mock interviews completed.
- Accuracy and efficiency in coding problem solving.
- Quality of system design articulation and tradeoff reasoning.
- Behavioral story readiness and interviewer comfort level.
- Number of final-stage interviews or offers achieved.

## Common mistakes
- Skipping foundational practice in favor of mock interview drills.
- Focusing only on coding and neglecting system design or culture-fit rounds.
- Not capturing learning after each practice session.
- Treating the process as a single event rather than a multi-week program.

## Expected outcomes
- A high-confidence FAANG interview preparation program.
- A polished technical and behavioral toolkit.
- Clear progress on problem-solving speed and communication.
- A defined offer evaluation framework for elite roles.
