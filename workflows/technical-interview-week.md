# Technical Interview Week

## Objective
Prepare for a technical interview week with a focused, practice-first schedule that covers coding, system design, and real-time execution.

## Target audience
- Engineers with upcoming technical interview rounds.
- Candidates who need a week-long sprint to sharpen coding and design skills.
- Anyone preparing for a condensed interview window across multiple companies.

## Recommended agents
- `career/technical-interview-coach.md`
- `career/resume-strategist.md`
- `career/hr-interview-coach.md`
- `career/offer-evaluation-advisor.md`

## Step-by-step process
1. Identify the technical interview formats and question types you expect.
2. Build a practice schedule with `technical-interview-coach` for coding, whiteboarding, and system design.
3. Use `resume-strategist` to ensure your resume supports the roles you are interviewing for.
4. Practice with real prompt-response rounds and post-mortem your performance.
5. Add behavioral prep with `hr-interview-coach` for the non-technical rounds.
6. Capture repeatable weaknesses and improve them daily.

## Weekly execution plan
Day 1: Diagnose strengths and weaknesses; plan the week.
Day 2: Focus on core algorithms and coding fluency.
Day 3: Practice system design and architecture thinking.
Day 4: Combine coding and design scenarios in mock interviews.
Day 5: Review feedback, polish communication, and prep final interview questions.
Day 6: Execute mock interview simulations with timed conditions.
Day 7: Rest, reflect, and finalize the interview readiness checklist.

## Success metrics
- Number of timed coding problems completed.
- Improvement in problem-solving speed and correctness.
- Confidence in system design tradeoffs.
- Clarity of technical explanations and reasoning.
- Readiness rating across all interview components.

## Common mistakes
- Focusing only on one interview format.
- Rushing through practice without capturing feedback.
- Ignoring the communication and explanation layer.
- Treating the week as a checklist instead of a learning loop.

## Expected outcomes
- A complete technical interview week playbook.
- Stronger coding and system design execution.
- Better ability to answer follow-up and clarifying questions.
- Higher confidence entering the actual interview window.
