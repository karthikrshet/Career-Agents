# Fresher Placement

## Objective
Turn graduation momentum into a first full-time role through polished materials, targeted search, and disciplined interview preparation.

## Target audience
- Final-year students preparing for campus placement or off-campus hiring.
- Recent graduates looking for their first software or tech role.
- Early-career professionals transitioning from internships to full-time employment.

## Recommended agents
- `career/placement-coach.md`
- `career/resume-strategist.md`
- `career/job-search-strategist.md`
- `career/technical-interview-coach.md`
- `career/hr-interview-coach.md`
- `career/linkedin-growth-advisor.md`

## Step-by-step process
1. Run a readiness audit with `placement-coach` to diagnose gaps in skills, documents, and timeline.
2. Build a role-targeted resume and job search narrative with `resume-strategist`.
3. Map a job search funnel using `job-search-strategist`: target companies, referral channels, and job boards.
4. Improve your LinkedIn and personal brand with `linkedin-growth-advisor` to attract recruiters.
5. Prepare for technical screens with `technical-interview-coach` and behavioral rounds with `hr-interview-coach`.
6. Track every application, follow-up action, and interview outcome.
7. Use `placement-coach` to prioritize the strongest roles and avoid unnecessary interview fatigue.

## Weekly execution plan
Week 1: Assess readiness, complete resume overhaul, and launch the top 10 applications.
Week 2: Build your application funnel, improve LinkedIn, and begin technical prep.
Week 3: Maintain outreach cadence, complete mock interviews, and refine interview scripts.
Week 4: Review offers, improve weak points, and finalize company priorities.

## Success metrics
- Number of quality applications submitted.
- Interview invitations earned per week.
- Confidence rating in technical and HR interview readiness.
- Offer velocity and offer quality relative to target roles.
- Degree of clarity on career direction and company fit.

## Common mistakes
- Applying to too many unrelated roles at once.
- Running a job search without a clear success metric or follow-up plan.
- Treating technical and HR prep as separate problems instead of a coordinated process.
- Ignoring recruiter outreach and network momentum in favor of job boards only.

## Expected outcomes
- A strong, coach-reviewed candidate package.
- A live job search funnel with interview-ready opportunities.
- Measurable progress toward first job placement.
- A repeatable system for closing the first full-time role.
