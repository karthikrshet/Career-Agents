# HR Interview Week

## Objective
Prepare for HR and behavioral interview week with structured storywork, company fit analysis, and decision readiness.

## Target audience
- Candidates facing multiple HR or recruiter conversations in one week.
- Professionals who need to quickly align stories to company values.
- Anyone who wants a repeatable behavioral interview system.

## Recommended agents
- `career/hr-interview-coach.md`
- `career/placement-coach.md`
- `career/offer-evaluation-advisor.md`
- `career/recruiter-outreach-specialist.md`

## Step-by-step process
1. Catalog the roles and companies you are interviewing with.
2. Use `hr-interview-coach` to build your core behavioral stories.
3. Align your stories to company culture, role expectations, and competency frameworks.
4. Prepare role-specific questions that demonstrate curiosity and fit.
5. Use `placement-coach` to map offer timing, next steps, and fallback options.
6. Create a decision criteria matrix with `offer-evaluation-advisor` for post-interview evaluation.

## Weekly execution plan
Day 1: Collect interview schedules and role requirements.
Day 2: Build and refine STAR stories for core behavioral themes.
Day 3: Practice answers with a focus on growth, collaboration, and conflict resolution.
Day 4: Prepare company-specific fit statements and questions.
Day 5: Review and rehearse final interview messaging.
Day 6: Run live mock HR conversations with timed responses.
Day 7: Debrief, capture feedback, and update your decision matrix.

## Success metrics
- Number of polished behavioral stories ready.
- Clarity of role fit and company motivation.
- Confidence in handling common HR questions.
- Effectiveness of follow-up questions and closing statements.
- Alignment of interview outcomes with offer criteria.

## Common mistakes
- Using the same generic story for every question.
- Focusing only on accomplishments instead of impact and learning.
- Ignoring the company’s values and culture in answers.
- Failing to prepare good questions to ask at the end.

## Expected outcomes
- A complete HR interview preparation plan.
- Stronger behavioral stories and interviewer alignment.
- Better clarity on which offers match your priorities.
- Faster, more confident closing conversations.
