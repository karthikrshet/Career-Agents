# LinkedIn Growth

## Objective
Turn LinkedIn into a career asset that attracts recruiters, signals your expertise, and amplifies your job search.

## Target audience
- Candidates who need a stronger recruiter-facing professional profile.
- Students and early-career engineers who want to build visibility before applications.
- Anyone who wants to use LinkedIn as a strategic part of their career operating system.

## Recommended agents
- `career/linkedin-growth-advisor.md`
- `career/resume-strategist.md`
- `career/job-search-strategist.md`
- `career/recruiter-outreach-specialist.md`

## Step-by-step process
1. Audit your current LinkedIn presence and identify gaps in headline, summary, experience, and keywords.
2. Use `linkedin-growth-advisor` to build a recruiter-facing narrative and optimized profile structure.
3. Align LinkedIn content with your resume and target roles using `resume-strategist`.
4. Build a content plan that supports your job search themes and signals your strengths.
5. Use `recruiter-outreach-specialist` to convert profile visitors into conversations.
6. Monitor profile views, connection requests, and recruiter messages to refine the profile.

## Weekly execution plan
Week 1: Refresh headline, summary, and key experience sections.
Week 2: Publish one role-relevant post and engage in 3 industry conversations.
Week 3: Reach out to recruiters and alumni with tailored messages.
Week 4: Review recruiter responses and improve profile content with data.

## Success metrics
- Increase in profile views and recruiter outreach.
- Number of messages from relevant employers.
- Alignment between profile narrative and target job descriptions.
- Growth in meaningful connections from target industries.
- Quality of recruiter inquiries received.

## Common mistakes
- Using vague or generic profile language.
- Trying to optimize for all audiences instead of target roles.
- Ignoring the recruiter perspective in favor of personal storytelling.
- Failing to reconnect your profile with actual interview outcomes.

## Expected outcomes
- A strong LinkedIn profile that supports your job search.
- Clear role positioning and keyword alignment.
- A measurable increase in recruiter engagement.
- A reliable platform for outreach and networking.
