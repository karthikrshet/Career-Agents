# Ambassador Program

The **Career Operating System Ambassador Program** is designed for student leaders, developer community organizers, and open-source advocates who share Career-Agents with their local networks.

---

## 🚀 Ambassador Responsibilities

1. **On-Campus Workshops**: Conduct developer sessions teaching peers how to use the Career CLI (`career-agents use`) with Cursor, Claude Code, and Copilot.
2. **Community Sourcing**: Help students package and validate final-year projects and mock interviews.
3. **Regional Syncs**: Host local hackathons and review local career readiness scores.

---

## 🎁 Ambassador Benefits

- **Exclusive Swag**: Career OS branded t-shirts, stickers, and notebook kits.
- **Career Mentoring**: Direct resume reviews and referrals from core project maintainers.
- **Featured Profile**: Displayed on the repository map and website directories.

---

## 📝 Apply Now
To apply to represent your university or city, open a GitHub issue with the template label `ambassador-application`. Describe your student club or developer group and list 2 ideas for local workshops.
