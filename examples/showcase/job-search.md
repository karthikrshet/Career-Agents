# Showcase: Job Search Case Study

This case study shows how an applicant used the **Career Operating System** to automate and scale their job search, converting applications from passive submittals into structured outbound pipelines.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Candidate Persona
- **Role**: Backend Developer (Spring Boot, Postgres)
- **Experience**: 2 Years (Mid-level)
- **Goal**: Transition from a local service company to a remote-first product startup.

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Execution Playbook

### Step 1: Run Recommender to Setup Strategy
The applicant queried the profile matcher:
```bash
career-agents recommend
```
- **Inputs**: `java, postgres, spring boot`, `mid`, `remote-job-bundle`, `backend-engineer`
- **Output Matches**: `Job Search Strategist`, `LinkedIn Growth Advisor`, `Remote Job Hunt Workflow`

### Step 2: Formulate Outreach Sequences
The applicant ran the `Recruiter Outreach Specialist` to build warm pipelines:
```bash
career-agents run recruiter-outreach-specialist
```
- **Simulated Action Item**: Built an email sequence targeting early-stage HR leads with a 3-sentence introduction showing immediate schema/architectural alignment.

### Step 3: Track and Monitor
The candidate logged target startups in their application dashboard, using outbound hooks and weekly trackers.

---

## 📈 Results
- **Active Outbound Conversions**: Out of 20 personalized manager outreach notes sent via LinkedIn, received **6 screening requests** (30% response rate vs 2% standard application portal average).
- **Target Role Landed**: Backend Software Engineer at a distributed remote-first SaaS company.
