# Who Uses Career OS?

The **Career Operating System** is designed for software developers, product builders, tech student leaders, and founders at various career stages.

---

## 👥 Primary User Personas

### 1. The University Student & Recent Graduate
- **Profile**: Computer Science or engineering students searching for final-year project guidance, internship opportunities, or initial job placement cycles.
- **Key Utilities**:
  - `fresher-placement` (workflow)
  - `internship-hunt` (workflow)
  - `final-year-project-advisor` (agent)

### 2. The Mid-to-Senior Engineer (FAANG Target)
- **Profile**: Experienced developers looking to optimize their compensation benchmarks, pass coding/behavioral interviews, or design scalable systems architectures.
- **Key Utilities**:
  - `faang-bundle` (bundle)
  - `system-design-coach` (agent)
  - `google-interview-coach` (agent)

### 3. The Career Switcher
- **Profile**: Professionals transitioning from non-technical backgrounds (e.g. Sales, Marketing, Retail) into tech.
- **Key Utilities**:
  - `career-switch-bundle` (bundle)
  - `career-pivot-to-tech-advisor` (agent)

### 4. The Early-Stage Builder (Founder)
- **Profile**: Startup founders and product managers seeking to validate MVPs, structure retainer pricings, audit competition, or scope feature pipelines.
- **Key Utilities**:
  - `startup-founder-bundle` (bundle)
  - `founder-advisor` (agent)
  - `market-research-analyst` (agent)
