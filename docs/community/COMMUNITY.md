# Community Directory

Welcome to the **Career-Agents** community! We are building the open-source infrastructure for career planning, resume engineering, system design prep, and interview loops.

Here is how you can connect with us, get support, and collaborate.

---

## 💬 Communication Channels

- **GitHub Discussions**: The central hub for sharing custom workflows, showing off your agent loading setups, and requesting CLI enhancements.
- **Discord Server**: Join our Discord for real-time developer syncs, peer mock-interviews, and direct coordination.
- **GitHub Issues**: Use issues solely for reproducible bugs in the CLI and schemas, or concrete agent improvements.

---

## 🗓️ Community Events

- **Developer Office Hours**: Every alternate Thursday at 17:00 UTC on Discord. We review upcoming roadmap items and check validation updates.
- **Mock Interview Practice**: Community-organized mock panels every Saturday. Connect with peers using the `mock-interviewer` agent guidelines.

---

## 🚀 Showcasing Your Work

Have you built a wrapper, automated application tracker, or custom LLM dashboard using our agents or search index?
- Open a PR to add it to [SHOWCASE.md](../../SHOWCASE.md).
- Share your landing role success stories in [SUCCESS_STORIES.md](../case-studies/SUCCESS_STORIES.md).
