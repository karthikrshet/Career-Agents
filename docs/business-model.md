# Sustainable Open-Source Business Model

This document outlines the monetization frameworks and partnerships designed to ensure the long-term sustainability of the **Career-Agents** platform.

---

## 🏛️ Monetization Channels

We balance open-source freedom with financial viability through 6 distinct pillars:

### 1. Developer & Corporate Sponsorships
- **Platform:** GitHub Sponsors and Open Collective integrations.
- **Model:** Corporate partners sponsor core maintenance in exchange for featured logo placements in sitemaps and documentation footers.

### 2. Community Donations
- **Platform:** Support models (e.g. BuyMeACoffee, Patreon).
- **Model:** Individual users and job-seeking alumni submit micro-donations as thanks for landing job offers.

### 3. Premium Agent Marketplace
- **Objective:** Host a secondary market for premium, deeply niche, or highly advanced enterprise coaching agents.
- **Revenue Share:** 80% to community developer, 20% commission to the platform treasury to fund infrastructure.

### 4. Enterprise Placement Support
- **Objective:** Partner with technical recruitment firms and enterprise HR teams.
- **Model:** Offer companies private, white-labeled Career OS instances loaded with custom recruitment metrics and specialized filters.

### 5. College Placement Partnerships
- **Objective:** Standardized licensing for universities and training institutes.
- **Model:** Annual subscription package providing custom dashboard metrics, admin reporting portals, and certification keys for student cohorts.

### 6. Verification & Certification Keys
- **Objective:** Paid testing loops for certification programs.
- **Model:** Nominal validation fees for issuing blockchain-verified Certifications (e.g. Certified Agent Engineer) that graduates link on their profiles.
